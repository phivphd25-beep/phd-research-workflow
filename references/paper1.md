# Paper 1: Fake News Detection on Social Media Using NLP

## 📚 Full Citation

Shu, K., Sliva, A., Wang, S., Tang, J., & Liu, H. (2017).
**Fake News Detection on Social Media: A Data Mining Perspective.**
ACM SIGKDD Explorations Newsletter.

## 🎯 Objective

To provide a comprehensive overview of fake news detection methods using data mining and NLP techniques.

## 🧠 Methodology

* Data collection from social media platforms
* Feature extraction: linguistic, user-based, and network-based features
* Machine learning models for classification

## 🔑 Key Findings

* Combining content and social context improves detection accuracy
* Fake news spreads faster than real news on social networks

## ⚖️ Strengths

* Comprehensive survey of techniques
* Covers both content-based and social-based approaches

## ⚠️ Limitations

* Limited discussion on deep learning models
* Dataset constraints

## 💡 Relevance to Project

Provides foundational knowledge and feature engineering ideas for fake news detection.
