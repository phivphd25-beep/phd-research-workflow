# Paper 2: LSTM-Based Fake News Detection

## 📚 Full Citation

Hochreiter, S., & Schmidhuber, J. (1997).
**Long Short-Term Memory.** Neural Computation.
(Used in later fake news detection models)

## 🎯 Objective

To introduce LSTM networks for handling sequential text data in NLP tasks.

## 🧠 Methodology

* Use of recurrent neural networks (RNNs)
* Memory cells to capture long-term dependencies in text

## 🔑 Key Findings

* LSTM effectively captures context in sequences
* Improves performance in text classification tasks

## ⚖️ Strengths

* Handles long-term dependencies
* Suitable for NLP applications

## ⚠️ Limitations

* Computationally expensive
* Requires large datasets

## 💡 Relevance to Project

Useful for implementing deep learning models for fake news classification.
