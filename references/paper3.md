# Paper 3: BERT for Fake News Detection

## 📚 Full Citation

Devlin, J., Chang, M. W., Lee, K., & Toutanova, K. (2019).
**BERT: Pre-training of Deep Bidirectional Transformers for Language Understanding.**
NAACL-HLT.

## 🎯 Objective

To introduce BERT, a transformer-based model for understanding language context.

## 🧠 Methodology

* Pre-training on large text corpora
* Fine-tuning for specific tasks like classification

## 🔑 Key Findings

* BERT achieves state-of-the-art results in NLP tasks
* Context-aware embeddings significantly improve accuracy

## ⚖️ Strengths

* High accuracy
* Deep contextual understanding

## ⚠️ Limitations

* Requires high computational resources
* Complex to train and fine-tune

## 💡 Relevance to Project

Can be used to build an advanced fake news detection model with improved performance.
